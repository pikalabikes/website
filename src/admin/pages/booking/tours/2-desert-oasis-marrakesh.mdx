"How to survive in the desert" is the theme of this bicycle tour. Guided by a local moroccan tourguide you will cycle trough the palmtrees and the desert like landscape to discover the hidden beauty's of the "Palmarais" oasis, close to Marrakech. Our guide tells you about the history of the Oasis and how the Moroccans have been surviving there the last 1000 years. You will also learn some tricks on how to survive in the desert yourself!

During the tour we will make several stops at special places such as an old traditional tadelact factory, and the old water irrigration system used to bring water from the atlas mountains to the oasis. Drinks and moroccan snacks are included.
