We provide free cycling lessons for girls and women. In our safety course we teach them how to ride the bicycle and experience the benefits of cycling too.

---

Cycling is a male dominated activity in Morocco. To support women empowerment we provide free, safe, private cycling lessons for girls and women. It provides them with freedom of mobility, and shows that if you push yourself, you can do things, that you never expected from yourself.
