The Dutch Cantal Bakker learned the power of cycling while teaching refugees in the Netherlands how to cycle. At an age of 24 she decided to move to Morocco to fight polluting scooters and cars make Marrakech the cycling Capital of Morocco.

---

Her parents and friends said she was crazy, but now 6 years later, Pikala has become one of the biggest social projects of Marrakech and has won multiple prestigious awards and nominations from i.a. United Nations WTO, Smart City, and the Ben Salah Foundation!
