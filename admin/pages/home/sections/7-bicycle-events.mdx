With our social bicycle events we stimulate cycling and improve the image of the bicycle.

#### Social events

Through free bicycle reparations for the less financially fortunate, we offer free bicycle reparation during our yearly “Bicycle Atelier”. This to stimulate safe cycling, and to use our funds to help those in need. We also provide social activities around environmental awareness.

Apart from social bicycle events, we provide commercial communication campaigns. For big brands like “Orange, DHL and Tui” we organize different branded campaigns.
